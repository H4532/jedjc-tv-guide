How to publish a change

  1. Open the content editor and sign in to GitHub as H4532.
  2. Edit the required title, description, URL, badge or active status.
  3. Do not remove commas or quotation marks from the JSON structure.
  4. Select Commit changes.
  5. Wait approximately one to three minutes and refresh the guest portal.
